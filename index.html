Why are we here?

This is my first webpage written completely by hand. I've gotten this far so things must be going ok. Bravo for me. I plan on learning various coding languages so I decided to start a reference page to keep track of things and maybe document my journey a little as well.

I am brushing up on some HTML, CSS, and learning Javascript at the moment but hope to learn this plus delve into Ruby on Rails so I can be a Full Stack Developer. Hopefully this site will help me gather my thoughts and maybe help others as well. Helping others is always good Karma. #namaste

A few things about the mind behind the mayhem:

  • My first computer was a Commodore 64 and I learned BASIC because it was all we had. I mainly made inappropriate word based games, but it was the coolest thing ever.
  • I'm considered a Xennial. Yeah, it's a thing now, look it up. I fall right on the cusp of the generational shift. Exciting, I know. I was the first to use the new wave of technology. I'm a geek at heart.
  • My first phone was a Moto Razr and it was cutting edge technology at the time. My best flip phone. We were the cool kids and life was grand.
